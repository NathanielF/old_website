Decision Boundaries

Despite sounding like the protagonist of rejected sci-fi script, the perceptron is just a lowly algorithm. Inspired by the model of neuronal triggering patterns, this simple linear classifier was designed to enact a pseudo-biological processing mechanism. As our brain receives and processes different inputs it unconsciously corrects for infelicities in rendering based on a weighting derived from past experience. The thought is that we are progressively conditioned for behaviour that expedites performance of perceptual and physical reflex.
